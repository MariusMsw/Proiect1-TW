LOCATION

Located in the north of the Capital, in an area that concentrates companies and businesses in the new technological fields, the Piccolo Residence Complex meets the needs of a young and dynamic audience to live near the workplace and to save time. The future residents of the Complex will have the opportunity to move outdoors and take their children to Herastrau Park, which is just an eight-minute walk away. For shopping, Lidl and Mega Image are just outside the Complex, and Promenada Mall is a two-minute drive away.
